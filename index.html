Welcome to HAU Athens Toastmasters! Toastmasters club No: 822866, founded 2005.

Speak better, Sound better, Lead better.

Communication and leadership skills go hand in hand. As a chartered club of Toastmasters International, we meet three times a month in a friendly, supportive environment to present prepared speeches, practice impromptu speaking, give feedback and help each other develop and grow as speakers and leaders.

The language used during our meetings is English.
